Profile

I'm Dr.Yami, I code web and games

My real name is Cuong Nguyen, I am currently an international student at Oulu University of Applied Sciences in Finland and learning Software Engineering. In web development, I mostly do the back-end work and some UX design. For front-end, I have some experience with Javascript, HTML/CSS and currently learning some digital design and vector graphics for my work.

Beside web development, I also am a game programmer who work at an indie game studio. I have a vast amount of experience in programming 2D games, especially J-RPG genre. For game engines, I mostly work with RPG Maker and Love2D, still learning for Unity3D with 2D feature.

In case of general programming, I know some programming languages like C++, C#, Ruby, Python, Lua, Javascript. Beside above two fields, I also learn PC software development with Qt.

In real life, I'm into many genre of music and currently learning to produce my own music, at least for my own games. I also like Japanese culture, especially anime/manga and relating things, that is the reason why I choose my favorite character as picture profile instead of my own face (still have a picture of myself in LinkedIn).

I hope people love my work.
